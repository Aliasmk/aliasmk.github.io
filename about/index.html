About Me

Hello! Nice to meet you!

I am currently a fourth year Mechatronics student at the University of Waterloo, planning to graduate in the spring of 2018. Through my education and personal projects I have fostered particular interests and skills in embedded electronics, hardware, and mechanical design. I am currently searching for opportunities related to these fields!

I also love developing new skills and hobbies in non-technical areas. I am currently studying Japanese. I am also preparing my plans for an indoor vegetable garden following my success doing so in 2018. Improvisational comedy is also a hobby of mine - one that I am lucky to have the opportunity to share with the amazing community at The Making Box in Guelph and Kitchener-Waterloo.
