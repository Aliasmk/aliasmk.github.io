About Michael Kafarowski

Hello! Nice to meet you!

I am currently a fourth year Mechatronics student at the University of Waterloo, planning to graduate in the spring of 2019. Through my education and personal projects I have fostered particular interests and skills in embedded electronics, hardware, and mechanical design.I am currently searching for career opportunities related to these fields!

Though technically minded at heart, I love learning new hobbies and developing new skills in non-technical areas. For instance, I am currently studying Japanese. I am also preparing for an indoor vegetable garden following my success doing so in 2018. Improvisational comedy is another hobby of mine - one that I am lucky to have the opportunity to share with the amazing community at The Making Box in Guelph and Kitchener-Waterloo.
