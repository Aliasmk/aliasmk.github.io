3D Printed Cell Phone Stand

For one of my first year university courses, we were challenged to design a cell phone stand on paper and then to solid model it using Solidworks. Finally, it would be 3D printed for us to keep. I enjoyed the introduction to the design process this project provided. Being able to finally hold a useful finished product in your hand is a satisfying way to end a project.
