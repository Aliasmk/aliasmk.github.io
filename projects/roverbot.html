Roverbot

Return to Projects

The Roverbot was the main deliverable for a project completed in a second year sensors and circuits course. Over a period of seven weeks my partner and I meticulously assembled and hand-soldered the robot using SMD and through-hole components. Each week's lab aimed to evolve the robot further, building in additional sensors and functionality. Powered by a PIC processor, the final robot contained the following sensors by the end of the course:

  • Thermistor (Temperature Sensor)

  • Hall Effect (Magnetic) Sensor

  • Wheel Encoders

  • Line Following IR transmitter and receiver
